INTRODUCTION                                    *jira*

This plugin's main purpose is to make working with Jira more pleasant.


COMMANDS                                        *editanywhere-commands*

Jira					      *:JiraBrowse*

        Open a Jira issue in a browser.

					      *:JiraLink*

        Open a Jira link in a browser.


MAPPINGS                                        *jira-mappings*

There are no mappings registered by the plugin.
You need to map it according to your preference.

ABOUT                                           *jira-about*
